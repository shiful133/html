Know Your Soil

Understanding soil is crucial for sustainable agriculture, as it guides proper nutrient management and irrigation practices, ensuring higher crop yields. Soil knowledge also supports environmental conservation by preventing erosion, pollution, and degradation. Moreover, it plays a key role in land-use planning and can help address global food security challenges.
